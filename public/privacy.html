Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

VitaLink (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the VitaLink mobile app and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, and login credentials.
  • Health Information (optional): Medications, doctors, and insurance cards you choose to store in the app.
  • Device Information: Non-personal details such as app version and device type, used for app improvement.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve VitaLink services.
  • Help you securely store and manage your health and insurance records.
  • Allow authorized insurance agents (with your consent) to access documents such as HIPAA and Scope of Appointment forms.
  • Communicate important updates, security alerts, or support messages.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your information. Your data is only shared when:

  • You give consent (e.g., sending forms to your insurance agent).
  • It’s required to comply with legal obligations.
  • It’s necessary to protect the security or integrity of VitaLink.

Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Sensitive records are protected both in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to you and those you authorize.

Your Choices

  • You may update or delete your account information in the app.
  • You may revoke agent access to your documents at any time.
  • You may contact us to request deletion of your account and all associated data.

Children’s Privacy

VitaLink is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with the new effective date.
